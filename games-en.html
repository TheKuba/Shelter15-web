The Cursed Crypt

The Cursed Crypt is a game developed by one of Shelter's members, TheKub4. The game is still in the early stages of development with the release date being unknown.
In the game, you will traverse procedurally generated dungeons, kill enemies, collect various upgrade items, and try to win against the demons that have cursed you.
